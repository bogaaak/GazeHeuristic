Gaze Heuristic Simulator

The simulation is based on the fighter vs. bomber example in Hamlin's (2017, in press) biography of the gaze heuristic.

  • Use the space bar to play / pause.
  • Reset to starting position using 'r'.
  • Try to fool the gaze heuristic and use 'n' (left) and 'm' (right) to steer the bomber.
  • The 'Tizzy angle' is determined once in the beginning based on the position of both airplanes and the bomber's initial direction. You can recalculate the 'Tizzy angle' at any time or set it manually. A 'Tizzy angle' of 0Â° corresponds to the 'AN method' as described in the paper and supposedly used by the German Luftwaffe in WW II.
  • The fighter's cycling rate corresponds to the number of frames the fighter has to wait before it can readjust its direction to keep the 'Tizzy angle' constant. A higher value thus makes the fighter thus more lethargic.
